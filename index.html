About the Solar System

Our solar system includes the Sun, eight planets, five dwarf planets, and hundreds of moons, and thousands of asteroids and comets. Our solar system is located in the Milky Way, a spiral galaxy. Our Sun is in a small arm of the Milky Way called the Orion Arm. Our solar system orbits the center of the galaxy at about 515,000 mph. It takes about 230 million years to complete one orbit around the galactic center.
